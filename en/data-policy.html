2. Legal basis for processing

Personal data is processed in accordance with Article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), based on the user’s consent or the legitimate interest of responding to inquiries.

3. Categories of personal data collected

Personal data is collected solely for the purpose of responding to user inquiries, managing professional communication, and ensuring the security of the website.

5. Data recipients

Personal data is not sold, rented or transferred to third parties. It may only be accessed by the data controller and, where applicable, by technical service providers bound by confidentiality obligations.

6. Data retention period

Personal data is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, and in compliance with applicable legal obligations.

7. Cookies and tracking technologies

This website does not use tracking cookies or profiling technologies. If such technologies are implemented in the future, users will be informed and their consent will be obtained where required by law.

8. Data security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration or disclosure.

9. User rights

Requests may be sent to:
